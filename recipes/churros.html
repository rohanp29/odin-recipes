Churros

Back to Recipes

Description: Crispy on the outside, soft inside — classic churros rolled in cinnamon sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine water, sugar, salt, and 2 tbsp oil. Bring to a boil then remove from heat.
  2. Stir in flour until mixture forms a ball.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or skillet to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Transfer dough to a piping bag fitted with a star tip.
  5. Pipe strips of dough into hot oil and fry until golden.
  6. Drain on paper towels and roll in cinnamon sugar mixture.
  7. Serve warm with chocolate sauce if desired.
