Mushroom Risotto

Back to Recipes

Description: Creamy and savory mushroom risotto perfect for a cozy dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups arborio rice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth, warm
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© onion and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add mushrooms and cook until tender.
  3. Stir in arborio rice and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the white wine and stir until absorbed.
  5. Add warm broth one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until absorbed before adding more.
  6. Continue until rice is tender and creamy (about 18-20 minutes).
  7. Stir in butter and Parmesan. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
